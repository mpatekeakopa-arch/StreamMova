3. Information We Collect

3.1 Information You Provide

  • Account profile details used to access StreamMova
  • Streaming preferences and configuration settings
  • Support messages or contact information submitted to us
  • Information you authorize from connected third-party platforms

3.2 Information Collected Through Platform Integrations

  • Facebook Page Data: Page ID, Page name, and Page access permissions
  • Stream Data: Live stream titles, descriptions, status, and stream identifiers
  • Engagement Data: Publicly available metrics such as views, likes, and stream performance data
  • Technical Data: Browser type, device information, IP address, and usage logs for security and service operation

4. Facebook Platform Data Usage

StreamMova integrates with the Facebook Platform to allow users to connect their Facebook account, view eligible Facebook Pages, select a Page, and publish live video content to the selected Page.

When you connect Facebook, StreamMova may access:

  • Basic Facebook profile information, such as name and user ID
  • Facebook Page ID and Page name
  • Page access tokens required to create and manage live video sessions
  • Public stream and engagement information necessary for displaying live stream status

How we use Facebook data:

  • To authenticate the user with Facebook
  • To allow the user to select a Facebook Page
  • To create and manage Facebook Live Video streams
  • To display selected Page information inside StreamMova

Important: StreamMova does not publish to a Facebook Page unless the user explicitly starts a stream from within the application.

5. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the StreamMova multi-streaming service
  • To authenticate users and connect third-party streaming platforms
  • To create and manage live streaming sessions
  • To display stream status, Page selection, and platform connection information
  • To improve service reliability, security, and user experience
  • To detect abuse, prevent fraud, and comply with legal or platform obligations

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information.

We may share information only:

  • With service providers that support hosting, infrastructure, analytics, or security
  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect StreamMova, Newles Technologies, users, or the public
  • With your explicit consent
  • In aggregated or anonymized form for reporting and service improvement

7. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as needed to provide StreamMova services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and maintain security records.

When data is no longer needed, we delete or anonymize it. Data deletion requests are handled within 7 days where technically and legally possible.

8. Data Deletion Instructions

You may request deletion of data associated with StreamMova at any time.

  • Contact us at sales@newlestech.co.ls with the subject line Data Deletion Request
  • Include the email/account identifier used with StreamMova
  • You may also remove StreamMova access directly from Facebook under Settings → Apps and Websites

After receiving a valid request, we will delete associated data within 7 days, unless retention is required by law or security obligations.

9. Your Rights and Choices

  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Withdraw consent for connected platform access
  • Disconnect third-party integrations from your platform account settings
