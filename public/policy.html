StreamMova Multi-Streaming Platform

1 Introduction

StreamMova ("we," "our," or "us") is a multi-streaming platform that allows users to connect and manage multiple streaming accounts simultaneously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our application through Facebook Developer Platform integration.

By using StreamMova, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use our application.

2 Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account credentials and authentication tokens for connected streaming platforms
  • User preferences, settings, and configuration data
  • Contact information when you communicate with our support team
  • Profile information from connected platforms (username, profile picture, etc.)

2.2 Information We Automatically Collect

Through Facebook integration and connected streaming platforms, we may collect:

  • Stream Data: Publicly available stream information including titles, descriptions, categories, and thumbnails
  • Public Engagement Data: Likes, views, followers, and other public interaction metrics
  • Chat Commands & Messages: Public chat commands and interactions from your streams
  • Stream Analytics: Viewer statistics, peak view times, and engagement patterns
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our application, features used, and session duration
  • Technical Data: Device information, IP addresses, browser type, and operating system

3 How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the StreamMova multi-streaming service
  • Retrieve, synchronize, and display your stream data across multiple platforms
  • Process and display public engagement metrics (likes, views, commands) in real-time
  • Enable cross-platform chat command functionality
  • Improve, optimize, and develop new application features
  • Communicate with you about updates, new features, and important notices
  • Monitor and analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Ensure platform security, prevent fraud, and detect misuse
  • Comply with legal obligations and platform requirements

4 Data Collection from Streaming Platforms

When you connect your streaming accounts to StreamMova, we access and process:

  • Public Stream Information: Title, description, category, tags, thumbnails, and stream status
  • Public Engagement Metrics: View counts, like counts, follower counts, share statistics
  • Public Chat Data: Commands, messages, and interactions from your public streams
  • Stream Status: Whether you are live, offline, or scheduled for streaming
  • Platform-Specific Data: Data made available through each platform's public API

Important: We only collect data necessary to provide our multi-streaming functionality and only access public information available through platform APIs. We do not access private messages or non-public user data unless explicitly granted permission for specific features.

5 Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data in the following circumstances:

  • With third-party service providers who assist in operating our application, hosting, analytics, and customer support (under strict confidentiality agreements)
  • When required by law or to comply with legal processes
  • To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property, and that of our users
  • With your explicit consent for specific sharing arrangements
  • In aggregated, anonymized form for analytical, research, or reporting purposes
  • During business transfers such as mergers, acquisitions, or asset sales

6 Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to:

  • Provide StreamMova services to you
  • Comply with legal obligations and regulatory requirements
  • Resolve disputes and enforce our agreements
  • Maintain business records for legitimate purposes
  • Support ongoing service improvements

We typically retain active user data for 24 months after account deactivation. Aggregated and anonymized data may be retained indefinitely for analytical purposes.

8 Security Measures

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit using TLS/SSL protocols

  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms

Disclaimer: While we implement industry-standard security measures, no electronic transmission or storage method is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.
